The two clocks

Every purchase runs on two timers.
Most trackers watch one.

The return window slams shut in days. The warranty bleeds out over years. Warden winds both the moment a receipt lands in the vault — and 30 days before a warranty dies, it tells you to test the product while a claim is still possible.

Inside the vault

Six mechanisms, zero servers.

01

On-device OCR

Camera or gallery in — merchant, date and total out. ML Kit runs locally; the photo never travels.

02

Test-before-expiry

A nudge 30 days before warranty death: plug it in, stress it, claim it — while someone else still owes you.

03

Claim pack

One tap bundles the receipt image and purchase details into a share-ready dossier for the counter argument.

04

Value protected

The dashboard totals every unit of currency still under guard. Watch coverage, not just clutter.

05

Biometric lock

Optional fingerprint gate on the vault door. Your spending history is not a shared document.

06

Portable by design

JSON backup export and import over the share sheet. Leave whenever you like — with everything.

The quiet part, loud

Your purchases are nobody's business.

Warden makes zero network calls in any core flow. Receipts, items and reminders live in a local SQLite database inside app-private storage. There is no account to create, no telemetry to opt out of, no cloud to breach. The vault is the phone in your pocket.
